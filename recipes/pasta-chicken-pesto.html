Weekend pasta

Description

This recipe is only appropriate for weekends, preferably at lunch on Saturdays. It's perfect for weekends for it is a simple recipe but with tons of calories and proteins. It is inspired by Italian cuisine but without all the hassle of many different complicated steps.

The recipe presented here is only valid for one person but it is easily scalable. Be aware that it won't probably fit in a single plate.

Ingredients

  • Spaguetti: 500g
  • Chicken breast: 400g
  • Eggs: 2
  • Potatoes: 2 (the size is up to you)
  • Pesto Calabrese, brand Barilla

Steps

  1. Boil 1 liter of water in a pot.
  2. Boil 750 ml of water in a smal pot.
  3. Wash the eggs and the potatoes.
  4. Once the smal pot starts to boil, add the eggs and potatoes. Leave it there for 30 mins.
  5. Once the other pot starts to boil, add the pasta.
  6. Cook the pasta to your preference.
  7. Boil 600 ml of water in another pot.
  8. Once the pasta is cooked, start cooking the chicken breast.
  9. Once the eggs and potatoes are ready, peel them.
  10. Start plating by first placing the spaguetti, eggs and potatoes.
  11. Add the pesto calabrese and start mixing it.
  12. When the chicken breast is ready, plate it.
  13. Enjoy.
