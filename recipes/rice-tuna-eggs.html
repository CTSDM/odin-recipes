Fast tuna with eggs

Description

Simple recipe for a quick lunch, but still filled with lots of nutrients.

Disclaimer: Not recommended for daily consumption.

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • Eggs: 2

Steps

  1. Wash the eggs and boil it.
  2. Boil the eggs.
  3. Open the canned tuna and filter its oil.
  4. Place the filtered tuna into a bowl.
  5. Once the eggs are boiled, peel them.
  6. Mix the eggs with the filtered tuna.
  7. Enjoy.
