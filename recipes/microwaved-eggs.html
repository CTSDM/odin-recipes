Microwaved eggs

Description

Perfect for everyday breakfast. You just need eggs, microwaved, and cooked rice. It can't get much simpler than this.

DISCLAIMER: If not done correctly, this can explode, either inside the microwave or in front of your face.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2
  • Either already cooked rice from past days or uncooked rice

Steps

With cooked rice

  1. Wash the eggs.
  2. Put some cooked rice in a small bowl.
  3. Crack the eggs and add it to the small bowl. Cover it with some more cooked rice.
  4. Place the bowl inside the microwave and start microwaving. The time really depends on your microwave parameters. Until you get the hang of it, I recommend 30 seconds, then 20, then 10, etc. Every time checking how is the egg.
  5. Enjoy.

Without cooked rice

  1. Wash the eggs.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. Wait until the rice is cooked before microwaving the eggs.
  4. Crack the eggs into a small bowl.
  5. Microwave it. The time really depends on your microwave parameters. Until you get the hang of it, I recommend 30 seconds, then 20, then 10, etc. Every time checking how is the egg.
  6. Enjoy.
